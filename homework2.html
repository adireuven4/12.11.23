Tomato pasta bake

Make this simple student pasta and tomato dish for a delicious, filling and budget-friendly supper.
It is great way to use up simple storecupboard ingredients.


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g can cherry or chopped tomatoes
  • pinch suger

preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan and fry for 5 min until softened and lightly golden.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to pack instructions. Heat the grill to high. Mix together the cheese and breadcrumbs.
  3. Drain the pasta and stir into the sauce. Tip in the frankfurters and spoon into a headproof dish.
